06 · Built for natural turn-taking

Realtime is not enough.
It has to feel responsive.

True interruption

Speech start stops every queued source immediately and truncates the model’s spoken turn.

One active response

Late deltas are ignored, preventing interrupted responses from leaking back into playback.

Semantic turn detection

The session recognizes conversational intent instead of depending on a fixed silence timer.

Design principle

The assistant should yield as naturally as a thoughtful person would.
